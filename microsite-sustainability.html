Sustainability

Parkway strives to be a leader in sustainable operations and is committed to reducing the consumption of valuable natural resources. We are focused on developing and operating more energy-efficient buildings throughout our portfolio, and when warranted, applying for LEED and ENERGY STARÂ® certifications.  

Innovative energy conservation, water usage and waste reduction initiatives; sustainable partners and suppliers, and the measurement and reporting of operational performance are just a few of the ways Parkway promotes sustainability throughout the portfolio. 

Register and Benchmark Property

We register and benchmark all properties with Energy Star.  Properties utilize the Energy Performance Rating score and carbon footprint data as the starting point for benchmarking sustainable operations. 

Electricity Usage & HVAC

Parkway closely monitors electrical usage and has implemented policies to reduce usage and costs for our tenants. We incorporate energy efficient lighting in all of our buildings. We monitor and control HVAC systems and have implemented policies to reduce weekend usage.

Waste Recycling Program

Parkway offers recycling programs at all of our properties. Please contact your management team for more information on the recycling programs offered in your building. Our recycling programs include but are not limited to the following products:

  • Paper
  • Lighting products
  • Batteries
  • Ballast
  • Plastic
  • Glass
  • Aluminum
  • Computers, phones

Water Conservation

We utilize water savings measures in building operations, tenant build-outs, and landscaping. Our buildings utilize many water saving features including:

  • Low flow urinals and toilets
  • Auto flushers for urinals and toilets
  • Auto sensors for faucets
  • Drip irrigation
  • Automated landscape equipment
  • Cooling tower meters for sanitary credits
  • Low maintenance landscaping
  • Flood stops

Housekeeping 

Our properties follow procedures and protocol for sustainable cleaning operations, and all janitorial contracts will include sustainableterms and specifications. 

Purchasing

Parkway has established procurement guidelines to ensure sustainable products are used in all of our buildings.  We utilize SiteStuff as our purchasing platform vendor in an effort to evaluate and track the purchase of sustainable products.
