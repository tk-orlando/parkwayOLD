About PKY

Performance is in the details

From the moment you step onto a Parkway property, you will know you have arrived at some place different. We pay attention to even the smallest of details with innovative office environments that allow you to focus solely on the performance of your business. With healthier facilities, top amenities and enhanced safety and security features, your office space is designed with productivity in mind.

A NYSE-listed real estate investment trust, Parkway owns and operates high quality office properties primarily in high-growth markets in the Sunbelt. As of July 1, 2014, Parkway owns or has an interest in 51 office properties located in eight states with approximately 18.3 million square feet of leasable. We also manage and/or lease approximately 11.1 million square feet for third-party owners.
