Signature Properties

3344 Peachtree

Located in the heart of the Buckhead submarket of Atlanta, Georgia, 3344 Peachtree is the office and retail portion of a 50-story, vertical mixed-use development with remarkable amenities and access not replicated elsewhere in the Atlanta market.

3344 Peachtree Road NE, Atlanta, Georgia 30326

Hearst Tower

One of the signature buildings in the Charlotte skyline, the 46-story Hearst Tower is Charlotte's fourth tallest building and contains 970,002 rentable sq. ft. The Hearst Tower features a high-rise element that combines horizontal bands of glass with strong vertical piers of stone and precast concrete. The materials used throughout the building are rich, evoking the art deco period with a color scheme of black, white, gray and bronze.

214 North Tryon Street, Charlotte, North Carolina 28202

Hayden Ferry Lakeside I & II

Hayden Ferry Lakeside I & II are part of the award-winning Hayden Ferry Lakeside master-planned, mixed-use project. The development is located on an iconic 43-acre site located on the south shore of Tempe Town Lake in downtown Tempe, Arizona-at the geographic center of Metropolitan Phoenix.

60-80 East Rio Salado Parkway, Tempe, Arizona 85281

Bank of America Center

As one of the most recognizable images in Downtown Orlando, Bank of America Center is located at the heart of the city - directly across from the Orange County Courthouse with immediate access to Interstate 4 and the 408 East-West Expressway. Bank of America Center offers a full array of amenities with panoramic views of Downtown.

390 N. Orange Avenue, Orlando, Florida 32801

Corporate Center Four At International Plaza

Corporate Center Four at International Plaza is Westshore's newest premier, GOLD LEED certified office building. This Class A, 8 story building with more than 250,000 square feet is immediately adjacent to International Plaza Mall and Tampa International Airport.

4301 West Boy Scout Boulevard, Tampa, Florida 33607

San Felipe Plaza

San Felipe Plaza is a 45-story, 980,472 square foot tower in the Galleria submarket. Designed by Richard Keating, the building was built in 1984 and features a three-story lobby with granite, marble, and wood finishes. The building has an on-site bank, fitness center, restaurant and convenient garage parking with approximately 2,700 covered spaces. The building offers unmatched panoramic views of Houston and beyond.

5847 San Felipe Street, Houston, TX 77057

CityWest Place

CityWestPlace is an innovative, master-planned Class A office complex that offers not only a superior location but an unusually broad spectrum of amenities including indoor and outdoor recreation facilities, restaurants and services. The four office buildings within the complex range from 6 stories to 21 stories and in aggregate comprise 1,475,000 rentable square feet. On-site parking is available in four structured parking garages. The exquisite 35.3-acre complex has received national recognition for its landscaping features. In addition, employees can take advantage of the many recreation options that include a Bermuda athletic field and jogging track, indoor basketball court, putting green, volleyball court, bocce ball court and horseshoe pits.

2101 City West Boulevard, Houston, TX 77042

San Jacinto Center

Located adjacent to Lady Bird Lake, San Jacinto Center features 21-stories of unparalleled views of downtown Austin, the Capitol, and Lady Bird Lake. Designed by Howard K. Smith Architects, the 410,248 square foot building is accented in rich Texas Crème limestone with Llano Rose granite details. The circular lobby features granite floors, walls clad in mahogany and limestone, and a coffered ceiling with mahogany details. On-site amenities include a conference room, ATM, an upscale restaurant, deli, fitness center, salon, and overnight courier drop box. The property also features in-building parking, with more than 640 subterranean spaces, as well as a convenient visitor surface lot.

98 San Jacinto Blvd, Austin, TX 78701

One Congress Plaza

Prominently located at First Street and Congress Avenue in downtown Austin, One Congress Plaza provides easy access to the State Capitol, Lady Bird Lake, and numerous hotels and restaurants. The 30-story tower features 518,385 of space and overlooks a one-acre plaza with a terraced lawn adorned with extensive landscaping and seating. The building’s exterior features a striking stair-stepped pyramid design with a red granite façade while the lobby features a patterned teak ceiling, polished marble glass floors, and a granite curtain wall. The adjacent parking garage provides 861 spaces and is connected by an underground walkway. On-site amenities include a conference center, deli, full-service bank, ATM, overnight courier drop box, fitness center, and salon. One Congress Plaza’s location provides easy access to the Capitol’s government and business centers, as well as Interstate 35 and Mopac Expressway.

111 Congress Avenue, Austin TX 78701

Courvoisier Centre

Courvoisier Centre is a two-building office complex with an adjacent five-story parking garage, located on Brickell Key Island in Biscayne Bay, 400 feet off the coast of Downtown Miami. The property totals 330,000 rentable square feet, including the adjacent five-story parking garage. Additional features include exceptional water views, several corner offices, exclusive balconies, banking facilities, a gourmet grocery, an on-site dry cleaner, and various retail amenities. Brickell Key Island is an exclusive, gated island containing high-end luxury condominiums and the five-star Mandarin Oriental Hotel and Spa. Courvoisier Centre is the only office complex on Brickell Key. Brickell Key is 400 feet off the coast of downtown Miami, has convenient access to Interstate 95, and is located eight miles from Miami International Airport.

501 & 601 Brickell Key Drive, Miami, FL 33131
